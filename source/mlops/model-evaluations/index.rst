Models evaluations
##################

Evaluating a machine learning model consists of computing its performance and behavior on a set of data called the Evaluation set. Model evaluations are the cornerstone of MLOps capabilities. They permit :doc:`/mlops/drift-analysis/index`, :doc:`/mlops/model-comparisons/index` and :doc:`automating retraining of models <automating>`
