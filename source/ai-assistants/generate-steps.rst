Generate Steps
###############

Generate Steps is an assistant that allows you to directly ask in natural language what you want to do in a Prepare recipe. The assistant then automatically generates your prepare steps.


.. seealso::
    
	For more information, see also the following article in the Knowledge Base:
    
    - `Concept | AI Prepare <https://knowledge.dataiku.com/latest/data-preparation/prepare-recipe/concept-ai-prepare.html>`_


Setup
======

The Generate Steps feature is not enabled by default.

Administrators can enable it under Administration > Settings > AI Services > Enable Generate Steps, Recipe, and Metadata.

This also enables:

* The :doc:`Generate Recipe <generate-recipe>` feature.
* The :doc:`Generate Metadata <generate-metadata>` feature.



Note: Generate Steps was previously called AI Prepare
