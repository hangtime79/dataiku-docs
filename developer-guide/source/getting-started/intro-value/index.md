# Introduction

```{eval-rst}
.. meta::
   :description: Dataiku is a centralized platform that empowers users to leverage Everyday AI
                 through capabilities like GenAI, machine learning, and analytics,
                 catering to both non-coders and coders for enhanced data management and insights.
                 It promotes efficient data preparation, AI governance, and operational management,
                 ensuring secure and scalable AI solutions for all levels of expertise within an organization.
```
Dataiku is a govern centralized platform for Everyday AI, based on seven key capabilities:

1. **GenAI & Agents**:
   Move beyond the lab and safely deliver GenAI & Agents at enterprise scale.
   Dataiku offers teams a secure large language model (LLM) gateway, no-code to full-code development tools,
   and AI-powered assistants to help everyone do more with GenAI & Agents.

   * For more information, **non-coders** can follow the {doc}`kb:getting-started/quick-starts/gen-ai/quick-start-index` (in the Knowledge Base).
   * **Coders** will find several tutorials in {doc}`/tutorials/genai/index` and small examples in {doc}`/concepts-and-examples/index`.

2. **Machine Learning**: From a guided approach with AutoML to cutting-edge techniques and full code,
   use Dataiku to build and evaluate machine learning (ML) models faster —
   all with the highest standards of explainability. 

   * For more information, **non-coders** can follow the {doc}`kb:getting-started/quick-starts/ml/quick-start-index` (in the Knowledge Base).
   * **Coders** will find several tutorials in {doc}`/tutorials/machine-learning/index` and small examples in {doc}`/concepts-and-examples/index`.

3. **Analytics & Insights**: Upgrade your business intelligence (BI) and self-service analytics efforts with Dataiku. 
   Enable everyone to make better, faster, everyday decisions built on trusted data via capabilities like visualization,
   dashboards, GenAI-powered storytelling, and more — all in one unified platform.

   * **Non-coders** can follow the {doc}`kb:data-viz/index` (in the Knowledge Base) for more information.
   * **Coders** will find tutorials about {doc}`/tutorials/webapps/index` and small examples in the {doc}`/concepts-and-examples/index` section.

4. **Data Prep for AI**: Connect, cleanse, and prepare data 10x faster with Dataiku. From data preparation, 
   effortlessly transition to anything from basic analysis to modeling and even deployment —
   all within a single environment. 

   * For more information, **non-coders** can follow the {doc}`kb:getting-started/quick-starts/data-prep/quick-start-index` (in the Knowledge Base).
   * **Coders** will find code samples in {doc}`/concepts-and-examples/index`,
     as well as instructions on how to customize their inputs in {doc}`/tutorials/plugins/index`. 

5. **AI Governance**: Enforce AI governance standards across all data work, all in one place.
   From data preparation and self-service analytics to machine learning and generative AI applications or projects,
   maintain visibility and reduce risk as your AI portfolio scales with Dataiku.
   
   **Coders** and **non-coders** will find a lot of information on their respective websites
   ({doc}`the Developer Guide</index>` and {doc}`the Knowledge Base<kb:index>`, respectively).

6. **AI Engineering Operations**: Manage all dimensions of your AI portfolio operations through a single, unified platform.
   Whether you’re automating data pipelines to ensure clean, reliable, and timely data or
   deploying and managing machine learning models and GenAI applications in production, 
   Dataiku brings all your project operations together seamlessly.

   **Coders** and **non-coders** will find a lot of information on their respective websites
   ({doc}`the Developer Guide</index>` and {doc}`the Knowledge Base<kb:index>`, respectively).

7. **AI Ecosystem**: With Dataiku, it’s easy to create analytic dashboards and data products and
   share them with business users to support day-to-day decision making.
   Generative AI applications, what-if analysis with outcome optimization,
   and interactive web apps — developed with or without code — are just a few ways to empower your organization with self-service analytics.

   **Coders** and **non-coders** will find a lot of information across their respective websites.
